Hello world!

My name is Almas and I am an independent environmentalist. I try to lead the right lifestyle and look after the place that I call my home. My home — is my planet. And I, as a resident of my house, must keep it clean and keep order in it. To which I urge you, my friends, not to remain indifferent, because we all live in one big house.

Founder

There is nothing that can stop me. I am inherent in movement and self-learning. And I am always glad when people come up to me with questions about how to make the world a better place or offer their ideas. I respect, appreciate, support and even sponsor such people, despite my age and bank account.
